# Creating a New Braintree Account

## Step 1: Sign Up
1. Visit https://www.braintreepayments.com/
2. Click **Sign Up** or **Get Started**
3. Choose your country/region

## Step 2: Business Information
Provide:
- Business name and type
- Business address
- EIN/Tax ID (or SSN for sole proprietors)
- Business category/MCC code

## Step 3: Owner Information
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- SSN (for US)
- Home address

## Step 4: Bank Account
- Routing number
- Account number
- Account type (checking/savings)

## Step 5: Verification
- Wait for email verification (usually 1-2 business days)
- May require additional documentation

## Step 6: Access Sandbox Immediately
While waiting for production approval:
1. Log into sandbox.braintreegateway.com
2. Start integration and testing

## Step 7: Production Approval
- Approval typically takes 2-5 business days
- You'll receive email notification
- Production credentials become available

## After Approval
Follow the [Existing Account Setup Guide](./existing-account.md)

---
*Note: Approval times and requirements vary by country and business type.*
